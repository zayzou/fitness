Nutrition Plan

Meal Timing and Composition

Time Meal Foods and Supplements
7:00 Breakfast 🍳 3 whole eggs, 🍳 3 egg whites, 🍳 80 g oats with 1 tbsp peanut butter, 🥜 1 banana, 🍌 250 ml milk, 🥛 multivitamin, 💊 Omega-3, 🧠 Vitamin D, 🌞 10 g collagen

19:30 Dinner 🍽 225 g salmon or 🍣 lean beef, 🍖 200 g quinoa or 🍚 sweet potato, 🍠 200 g steamed vegetables, 🥦 1 tbsp olive oil
21:30 Evening Snack 🥄 Cottage cheese with 🍯 1 tbsp honey, 🍯 handful of walnuts

Supplementation Schedule

Time Supplement Amount
Daily Creatine 💪 5 grams (💪 mixed with post-workout shake or 🥤 taken with water/juice any time of the day)
Post-Workout Whey Protein 1-2 scoops (20-40 grams)
Daily Collagen 10 grams (mixed in morning coffee or any beverage)
Morning Multivitamin As per label instructions
Morning Omega-3 1-2 grams
Morning Vitamin D 1000-2000 IU (depending on your current levels and doctor's advice)
Pre-Workout Caffeine 200 mg (adjust based on tolerance)
Pre-Workout Beta-Alanine 2-3 grams
Pre-Workout BCAAs (optional) 5-10 grams (during workout, optional for muscle recovery)
